World Wide Generation

I was given the responsibility to develop a straightforward and visually engaging representation that outlines uncomplicated steps individuals can take to enhance their ecological practices. As you can see on the right. This illustration aims to simplify and promote easy-to-follow actions for adopting a more environmentally conscious lifestyle.

The Sustainable development goals believe that we should achive the following to achive this:
Minimize waste by opting for reusable items. Sort and recycle materials like paper, plastic, and glass. Conserve Water:
Fix leaks promptly. Use a broom instead of a hose to clean driveways. Save Energy:
Choose walking, cycling, or public transport. Consider carpooling or using electric vehicles. Shop Responsibly:
Support local and eco-friendly products. Bring reusable bags and containers. Plant Trees and Greenery:
Participate in community tree-planting events. Grow plants at home for better air quality. Reduce Meat Consumption:
Opt for meatless meals. Choose sustainably sourced and local meats. Educate Others:
Share eco-friendly tips with friends and family. Participate in community workshops or events. Conserve Wildlife:
Avoid single-use plastics to protect marine life. Support wildlife conservation organizations. Compost Organic Waste:
Create a compost bin for kitchen scraps. Use compost to enrich soil for plants.
I tried to incorpate as many of these as possible while still keeping it simple.
