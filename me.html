This is Aide Ohirein

Pronounced: i-day.

Pronouns: She/Her.

Aide is a London based designer with experience in Motion/Animation, Graphics, and Illustration.

Her design style follows the K.I.S.S. principle, usually clean and simple. She has been freelancing for the last few years, has a true passion for content creation, she loves to use mixed media, she says it's because she needs to take inspiration from as many places as possible. It’s obvious that design is her true passion and that she prides herself on the desire to learn. For instance, she learned Figma, HTML, and CSS, to build this portfolio site.

She's a self-starter, and team player with a “can-do” ambitious attitude. In the next 5 years, she hopes to be a Creative Director and is actively taking the steps to become one.

    Adobe Photoshop
